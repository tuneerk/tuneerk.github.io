The Story So Far

My journey is fueled by a dual passion: the intricate logic of AI and the evocative art of photography. Starting with an engineering background from NIT Jalandhar and early explorations in data science, I honed my skills in Deep Learning and On-Device AI at Samsung, contributing to research and developing intelligent user experiences. Now, as an AI Engineer at Google, I build end-to-end ML solutions with GCP, continually driven to solve complex problems by blending analytical precision with a creative perspective, whether I'm crafting algorithms or capturing moments through my lens.
